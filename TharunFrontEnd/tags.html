Perplexity PERPLEXITY AI is a metric in information theory and machine learning that measures how well a probability model predicts a sample, indicating the models uncertainty—the higher the perplexity,the more uncertain the predictions. In natural language processing, it quantifies how “surprised” a language model is by new text, with lower perplexity meaning the model is more confident and accurate in predicting the next word.
